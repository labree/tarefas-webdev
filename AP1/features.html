Game Modes

Domination

Battle for control of strategic points across iconic Star Wars locations. Capture and hold these points to earn points for your team. The team with the most points at the end of the match wins!

Capture the Flag

Infiltrate enemy territory, steal their flag, and return it to your base while defending your own flag from enemy players. Team coordination and strategy are key to victory in this classic game mode.

Escort the Payload

One team must escort a valuable cargo across the map while the other team tries to stop them. The payload moves only when attackers are nearby, creating intense battles around the objective.

Team Deathmatch

The classic team-based combat mode. Work with your teammates to eliminate the opposing team. The first team to reach the score limit wins. Perfect for honing your combat skills!
